About our restaurant

Our Journey in the gastronomic world
On a quiet street in Seoul, among the colorful houses and lampposts adorned with lanterns, stood Soju & Spice. This Korean restaurant was as charming as it was unusual, as on its facade hung a sign with the cute figure of a koala, the place's adorable mascot. It was a special place, where chef Mi-na, passionate about Korean cuisine and Australian fauna. Decided to fulfill her dream of combining the flavors of her homeland with the exoticism and sweetness represented by Australia's friendly animals.


Thus, Soju & Spice became a gastronomic oasis, where customers were welcomed by a welcoming environment, decorated with soft tones and details that recalled both Korea and Australia. The delicious aroma of traditional Korean dishes mixed with the sweetness of the environment, creating a unique and irresistible atmosphere. Tables were adorned with tablecloths printed with a cute koala face and colorful napkins, while stuffed animals of this adorable marsupial appeared in strategic corners, bringing smiles to customers, young and old.
